‚ûï Adding 5
The +5 facts all relate to 10!

Think of it this way: 5 is half of 10.
So 5 + 5 = 10. That's the anchor fact!
5 + 5 = 10
This is the key fact. Memorize it!
6 + 5 = 11

7 + 5 = 12

‚ûï Doubles

5 + 5 = 10

7 + 7 = 14
8 + 8 = 16
